Frequently Asked Questions

Our services are designed for all job seekers, with special accommodations for persons with disabilities. Whether you're entering the workforce for the first time, changing careers, or returning after a break, our coaching can help. We have specific resources for people with visual, hearing, mobility, and cognitive disabilities.
Our basic job coaching services are completely free. We offer premium one-on-one coaching sessions at subsidized rates for those who need more personalized attention. Scholarships are available for those who cannot afford the fees - no one is turned away due to financial constraints.
We prepare candidates for a wide range of jobs across sectors including IT, retail, hospitality, banking, healthcare, and government services. Our training modules cover both general employability skills and sector-specific knowledge. We also work with employers to create inclusive opportunities matching your skills.
All our materials are designed with accessibility in mind. Text content is screen-reader friendly, videos include audio descriptions, and we provide alternative text for all images. You can also request materials in Braille or access our audio-only versions of all training modules.
