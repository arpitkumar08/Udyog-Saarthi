Interview Preparation Toolkit

General Questions

Tell me about yourself

How to answer: Focus on your professional journey, key skills, and achievements relevant to the position. Keep it concise (1-2 minutes).

Tip: Structure as Present-Past-Future

Behavioral Questions

Describe a time you faced a challenge

Use the STAR method:

  • Situation: Set the context
  • Task: Your responsibility
  • Action: Steps you took
  • Result: Positive outcome
